Our Mission

We here at Listen LA are a Los Angeles based community organization dedicated to amplifying LGBT Voices and Voices of Color, and do so with pride and solidarity for ALL members of our community. Sharing stories through the arts to raise awareness of social issues and inequalities.

We are founded in support of the Black Lives Movement and our efforts go to keeping the momentum of this movement alive through storytelling, performances and all manners of expressions of solidarity.

Through Listening we Understand, and Through Understanding we Heal.

We look forward to meeting you.

Again thank you!
